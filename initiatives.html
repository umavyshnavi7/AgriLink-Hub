Active

Benefit: Crop insurance against natural calamities
Premium: 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi crops
Coverage: Full sum insured for crop loss

Active

Benefit: Easy credit up to ‚Çπ3 lakh at 4% interest

Active

Benefit: Free soil testing and nutrient recommendations

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)

Benefit: Subsidy on drip/sprinkler irrigation (up to 90%)
Purpose: Water conservation and efficient irrigation
Coverage: All categories of farmers
